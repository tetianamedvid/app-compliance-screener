# Wix Payments: Prohibited Products and Services
# Source: https://support.wix.com/en/article/wix-payments-prohibited-products-and-services
# This is the full prohibited list for Base44 Payments powered by Wix.

## Adult
- Sexually oriented dating services
- Entertainment: gentleman's clubs, topless bars, strip clubs, sexually oriented massage parlors, saunas, escort agencies, sexually-related services
- Pornographic content: books, magazines, audio, videos, websites, streaming services
- Fetish products
- Adult goods (excluding fetish products)

## Financial businesses
- Cash advances by non-financial institutes
- Virtual currencies
- Check cashing
- Foreign Exchange
- Money orders
- Traveler checks

## Healthcare
- Nutraceuticals solely focused on weight loss or increasing sexual performance
- Nutraceuticals that are harmful or make unsupportable claims
- Supplements containing prescription ingredients and/or CBD/THC
- Pharmaceuticals, prescription medicine
- Medical benefit packages
- Intravenous therapy (IV drip bar, vitamin infusions, hangover cures)
- Health practitioners, private medical practices, e-doctors, dental/medical services
- Dental supplies
- Prescription-only and regulated medical devices (including contact lenses)
- Shell banks

## Intellectual Property (IP)
- Products to circumvent copyright protection
- Essay mills, paper mills (intent to falsely submit as own work)
- Counterfeit products, replicas, designed IP infringements

## Marketing practices
- Pyramid selling, chain letters, financial scams
- Products/services promoting hate, violence, discrimination, terrorism, harassment, abuse
- Inbound telemarketing for postcard/mailings
- Fake references and deceptive products
- Deceptive marketing tactics (false claims, before/after claims, misleading endorsements, cancellation avoidance, pre-checked opt-ins, misappropriation of incentives, sweepstakes)
- Data pass
- Excessive chargeback evasion practices
- Ransom or extortion-like practices
- Get rich quick schemes

## Not for profit
- Crowdfunding
- Unregistered charities

## Hazardous materials (B2B)
- Toxic, flammable, radioactive materials
- Bacteria cultures containing E. coli
- Peptides, research chemicals
- Nitric acid
- Prohibited ozone-depleting substances (ODS)
- Products containing cyanide
- Hydrofluoric acid

## Regulated (non-financial) products and services
- US-based gambling services
- Weapons, ammunition, military arms, explosive devices, firearm parts
- Fireworks, flammable or radioactive materials
- Tobacco, e-cigarettes, e-liquids
- Drug production tools/ingredients
- Legal gambling/games of skill with cash prizes (casinos, poker, bingo, slots, betting, lotteries, racing, fantasy sports)
- Illegal products/services, peripheral support of illegal activities
- Drugs, drug paraphernalia, illegal drugs, psychoactive products
- Cannabis dispensaries and related businesses
- Alcohol sales as main product (shops, bars, breweries, wineries)
- Veterinary practices

## Securities and trading
- High-risk securities (trading, crypto options, forex options, ICOs, spread betting, CFDs)
- Security brokers/investments (financial instruments, precious metals, options, shares, commodities, derivatives, currencies, securities)

## Third party payment processing
- Aggregators (platforms, marketplaces)
- Payment service companies (commissary accounts, bill payments, peer-to-peer)

## Technology
- Decryption/descrambler products
- Devices to block, jam or interfere with communications
