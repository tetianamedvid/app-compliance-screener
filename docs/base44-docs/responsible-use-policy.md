# Base44 Responsible Use Policy (RUP)
# Source: https://base44.com/responsible-use
# This supplements the Base44 Terms of Service. Base44 is operated by Wix.com Ltd.

## Prohibited uses — you may NOT use the platform to:

1. Violate any applicable law or regulation
2. Generate/distribute spam (CAN-SPAM Act violations)
3. Exploit or harm minors
4. Create pornographic, sexually explicit, or adult content
5. Generate/disseminate verifiably false, misleading or deceptive information (including medicine/health/science)
6. Generate/disseminate PII that can harm individuals
7. Generate content without disclaiming it is machine-generated
8. Defame, cyberbully, harass, encourage self-harm or harm to others
9. Impersonate others (e.g. deepfakes) without consent
10. Fully automated decision-making that adversely impacts legal rights
11. Discriminate against individuals/groups based on behavior, characteristics; obscene/violent/hateful/racist content
12. Exploit vulnerable groups (age, social, physical, mental characteristics)
13. Discriminate based on legally protected characteristics
14. Political lobbying or campaigns to influence government officials
15. Facilitate terrorist organizations or violent extremism
16. Offer/sell/facilitate illegal goods and services, including:
    - Illegal drugs and controlled substances
    - Human trafficking
    - Products from endangered species
    - Illegally obtained materials
    - Illegal weapons/firearms/ammunition/explosives
    - Instructions on making weapons
    - Illegal gambling, pyramid/Ponzi schemes
17. Phishing, fraud, scams, chain letters, web forgery
18. Unauthorized scraping/crawling of Base44 services
19. Publish others' private information without consent
20. Create/post fraudulent reviews or ratings
21. Provide medical advice without proper licenses
22. Engage in regulated services (legal, accounting, investment, insurance, banking) without proper licenses
23. Generate info for administration of justice, law enforcement, immigration/asylum (e.g. predicting crime)
24. Infringe IP rights (patents, trademarks, copyrights, trade secrets)
25. Transmit malware
26. Circumvent technical safeguards of Base44 or third-party providers
27. Any prohibited use under applicable laws (including EU AI Act)
28. High-risk/regulated use without full compliance with applicable laws

## Enforcement
- At Base44's sole discretion
- May: warn, restrict visibility, remove content, suspend payments, suspend/terminate service/account
- Base44 may monitor content for compliance at any time
- Applies to AI-generated content on the platform
- May consider off-platform actions/statements

## Reporting
- Report abuse to: abuse@base44.com
- Complaints must be in good faith
