# Setting up Base44 Payments powered by Wix
# Source: https://docs.base44.com/Setting-up-your-app/setting-up-wix-payments.md

Base44 Payments powered by Wix is Base44's built-in payment provider that lets you go from idea to selling in minutes.

## Availability
- Currently available only to eligible customers in the **United States**.
- Requires a **Builder plan** or higher.
- Must have a U.S.-based business and U.S. bank account.
- App must be published and accessible during verification.

## Accepted payment methods
- Major credit and debit cards
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- PayPal

## Prohibited products and services
To keep payments safe and compliant, some products and services **cannot be sold** via Base44 Payments powered by Wix:
- Illegal products or services
- Certain financial services, investment schemes or high-risk lending
- Some adult content or services
- Weapons, some types of ammunition and related accessories
- Certain gambling or betting services
- Products that encourage hate or violence

The exact list can change over time and may depend on Wix's banking and payment partners.
Full policy: https://support.wix.com/en/article/wix-payments-prohibited-products-and-services

## Setup flow
1. Connect Base44 Payments using the AI chat in your app editor.
2. Publish your app and go live.
3. Start accepting payments from customers.
4. Complete setup and verification to start receiving payouts.

## Verification requirements
- Business type and legal name
- Business address in the United States
- Main contact person's details
- Bank account where you want to receive payouts
- Supporting documents may be required

## Business requirements for apps
Before accepting payments, apps must include:
- Business name and contact details (legal name or DBA)
- Physical business address (not P.O. box)
- Customer service contact (email, phone)
- Terms and conditions
- Privacy policy
- Refund/return/cancellation policy
- Shipping/fulfillment/delivery policy
- Clear product/service descriptions
- Clear pricing and currency
- Transparent subscription/recurring payment terms

## Donations
- Cause must be registered as a charity in the United States
- May not accept donations on behalf of someone else
- Donations must be tied to a specific charitable purpose
- Does not support non-registered charities, crowdfunding, donation aggregations

## Payouts
- Standard delay of 2-5 business days
- Schedule: daily, weekly, or monthly
- Account must be fully verified before payouts begin
- Failed payouts shown with explanation in dashboard

## Refunds
- Issued from Payments dashboard
- Deducted from Base44 Payments balance
- If balance insufficient, taken from upcoming payments
- May take several business days to reach customer
